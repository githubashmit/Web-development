About us

Little Flower School, Rapti Nagar Phase - 4, Chargawan, Gorakhpur, is an English Medium School, established by the Little Flower Mission, Gorakhpur, a registered Society. The school is situated in Rapti Nagar Phase - 4, Chargawan, eight kilo metres away from the Gorakhpur Railway Station, on the Gorakhpur-Maharajganj Road. Though in the centre of the Rapti Nagar Housing colonies Phase I, II and IV, the school has a calm and quite atmosphere, away from the din of the city. The School was established in 1991, the birth centenary year of Fr. Basilius CST, founder of the Little Flower Congregation, the parent society of the Little Flower Mission that governs the school. The first classes started on 2nd July 1991, with 33 children in LKG and two teaching staff and one non-teaching staff. Now the school has become a full-fledged Inter College. The School is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi.
